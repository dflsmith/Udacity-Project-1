Dave's Favorite Movies

Matrix

Rating: R
Keanu Reeves, Laurence Fishburne, Carrie-Anne Moss
A computer hacker learns from mysterious rebels about the true nature of his reality and his role in the war against its controllers.

Matrix Reloaded

Rating: R
Keanu Reeves, Laurence Fishburne, Carrie-Anne Moss
Neo and the rebel leaders estimate that they have 72 hours until 250,000 probes discover Zion and destroy it and its inhabitants. During this, Neo must decide how he can save Trinity from a dark fate in his dreams.

Matrix Revolutions

Rating: R
Keanu Reeves, Laurence Fishburne, Carrie-Anne Moss
The human city of Zion defends itself against the massive invasion of the machines as Neo fights to end the war at another front while also opposing the rogue Agent Smith

The Bourne Identity

Rating: PG-13
Franka Potente, Matt Damon, Chris Cooper
A man is picked up by a fishing boat, bullet-riddled and suffering from amnesia, before racing to elude assassins and regain his memory

The Equalizer

Rating: R
Denzel Washington, Marton Csokas, Chloe Grace Moretz
What do you see when you look at me?

John Wick

Rating: R
Keanu Reeves, Michael Nyqvist, Alfie Allen
An ex-hitman comes out of retirement to track down the gangsters that took everything from him
